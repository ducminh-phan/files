Payment Options

You have your choice of several online payment methods:

  • American Express
  • MasterCard
  • Visa
  • Discover
  • PayPal (continental U.S. only)
  • Apple Pay (U.S. only)
  • AfterPay
  • Union Pay
  • JCB
  • Tory Burch Gift Card (U.S. only)
  • Tory Sport Gift Card (U.S. only)

We do not accept checks, cash, Amex Gift Cards, Visa Gift Cards, or money orders online.

Using Promo Codes 

Have a promo code? To redeem, please enter the code in the space provided on the Shopping Bag page and hit the "checkout" button. The value of the promotion will then be applied to your order at checkout.

Using Multiple Credit Cards

Unfortunately, we are unable to split payment by multiple credit cards at this time.

International Payments

We accept most credit cards with an international billing address. Tory Burch and Tory Sport Gift Cards are not accepted for international orders. For the list of countries where we deliver, see International Shipping.
