Client Services

Return Policy

As always, we gladly accept returns and exchanges of new, unused merchandise accompanied by a receipt free of charge. Returns and exchanges must be made within 30 days of receiving your order. We will accommodate a one-time price adjustment on full-price items within 14 days. Private Sale, sample sale, select outlet items and monogrammed products are final sale.

Extended Holiday Return Policy: For gifts purchased in November, you can exchange or return for a full refund if you ship your return no later than January 7, 2023. For items purchased outside this time frame, our standard return policy applies. 

Refund Policy

You will receive an email confirmation once we receive your return (note this email confirmation will be delayed), and you can expect your refund within 14 business days of that communication — issued in the form of the original payment or a Gift Card. Please allow 1 to 2 billing cycles for your account to be credited. PayPal exchanges must be even — any balance will need to be charged to a credit card. We are unable to process exchanges for international orders.

For returning an item purchased with a Gift Card, the refund is issued as an eGift and emailed as soon as the return is processed. We no longer issue a Physical Gift Card for refunds unless it is a for a gift recipient and the email is unknown.

Merchandise must meet our requirements for return:

  • Full-price and sale items in their original condition, with all tags attached, may be returned for a full refund or exchanged within 30 days of delivery
  • Final-sale items cannot be returned or exchanged
  • Outlet-store items may be returned within 14 days of delivery to outlet stores only — they are not returnable in full-price retail stores
  • Private Sale, sample sale, select outlet items and monogrammed products are final sale
  • Worn, altered or washed items cannot be returned or exchanged
  • In-store purchases cannot be returned or exchanged at toryburch.com
  • Items purchased at a department store or any other store selling Tory Burch merchandise can be returned only to that store
  • We reserve the right to deny a refund if returned merchandise does not meet our requirements

For your convenience, we offer free exchanges within the continental U.S. Start your exchange online by visiting toryburch.com/returns. You can either: (1) print your prepaid return shipping label, or (2) have FedEx print it for you when you bring your return code to a FedEx location. Next, package your item(s) and your packing slip. Aftepay orders cannot be exchanged. Need any additional assistance, contact Client Services. 

 

 

When you use PayPal, we will refund you by the following:

  • Online returns are refunded to your PayPal account
  • In-store returns are refunded with a Gift Card
  • We only accept even exchanges for PayPal returns — an additional form of payment or credit card is required for uneven exchanges. We are unable to process exchanges for international orders.

As always, we gladly accept returns of new, unused merchandise accompanied by a receipt free of charge. Returns must be made within 30 days of receiving your order. Orders purchased online with Afterpay must be shipped back to our warehouse for a refund and cannot be returned in-store.  Afterpay orders cannot be exchanged.  

To start a return, click here.

While Afterpay returns will follow Tory Burch’s standard return process, Afterpay refunds are slightly different than what you might be used to when using a traditional payment method.  Once the refund is processed by Tory Burch, it automatically drops through to Afterpay and your payment installment plan is adjusted to reflect the new total order value starting with the last payment installment and working backwards.

Visit Afterpay Returns and Refunds for more information on how refunds work at Afterpay.

You will receive your refund in the original form of payment or receive an eGift. Once we receive your return, we will send you an email confirmation and process your refund within 14 business days. Your bank may require additional time to post this transaction to your account. Please allow 1 to 2 billing cycles for your account to be credited.

Price Adjustments

We will gladly accommodate a one-time price adjustment on full-price items within 14 days of the order date. To receive a price adjustment, please contact Client Services and refer to the order number on your packing slip or order confirmation email. Private Sale items are excluded and other promotions may also be excluded.

Shipping Errors & Damaged Products

Your happiness with your order is our number one priority. In the unlikely event that we process an order incorrectly or you receive a damaged or defective item, we will gladly replace it or accept it for a full refund at our expense. Tory Burch will provide a prepaid FedEx shipping label to expedite returns within the U.S. To receive your shipping label and arrange to replace your product, please contact Client Services. Tory Burch customers using a third-party shipping service are responsible for any duties, taxes and shipping costs that may occur. Tory Burch is not liable for any damages from a third-party shipping service, or lost items or packages.
