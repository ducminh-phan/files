Expedited Shipping & Order Processing Time

Need something quickly? If you place your order before 2 PM ET Monday to Friday (excluding holidays), we begin processing the same day. If you place it after 2 PM ET, we begin processing the next business day. We process all orders within 1 to 2 business days, after credit card verification.

Shipping Fragrance

Due to shipping regulations, fragrance cannot be shipped via expedited shipping methods. Fragrance is shipped via ground separate from the other items in your order. We are unable to ship fragrance to international addresses, Alaska, Hawaii, Puerto Rico or APO/FPO addresses.

Shipping to P.O. Boxes & Military Addresses

Currently, we do not ship to P.O. boxes or APO/FPO military addresses.

Shipping Outside the Continental U.S. 

We ship to 18 countries from toryburch.com through our partner FedEx Cross Border — for complete details, see International Shipping.

Shipping to Alaska, Hawaii & Puerto Rico

For deliveries to Alaska and Hawaii, select Two Day or Next Day air services for expedited delivery. Deliveries to Puerto Rico will be shipped via FedEx Cross Border; the shipping cost is determined at checkout and will vary based on freight fees.  We are unable to ship pre-order items to Alaska, Hawaii or Puerto Rico.

Delivery Policy

If you refuse delivery of your order, it will be returned to our distribution center and you will receive a refund for the merchandise and tax paid. Shipping charges are nonrefundable.
