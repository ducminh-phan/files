Client Services

Start a Return or Exchange

As always, we gladly accept returns and exchanges of new, unused merchandise free of charge, within 30-days.

Extended Holiday Return Policy: For gifts purchased in November, you can exchange or return for a full refund if you ship your return no later than January 7, 2023. For items purchased outside this time frame, our standard return policy applies. 

Return and Refund Policy

For your convenience, we offer free returns and exchanges within the continental U.S. Start your return or exchange online by visiting toryburch.com/returns. You can either: (1) print your prepaid return shipping label, or (2) have FedEx print it for you when you bring your return code to a FedEx location. Next, package your item(s) and your packing slip.  AfterPay orders cannot be exchanged. Need any additional assistance, contact Client Services. 

International & Puerto Rico

Please contact Client Services with any questions. For more details, visit International Returns.

For quick returns and exchanges, bring your invoice or receipt (required) to any Tory Burch store. PayPal returns are refunded with a Gift Card. In-store purchases must be returned to a store. Afterpay orders cannot be returned in-store and must be shipped back.
