How can I learn more about kidney disease?

In addition to the Education section available on DaVita.com, you can learn about kidney disease through Kidney Smart®. Kidney Smart provides an online interactive learning program, self-paced program as well as in-person classes taught by kidney care professionals in neighborhoods throughout the country.
