Verizon Up FAQs

Learn about the new Verizon Up, free and available when you qualify with your mobile account.

Get Verizon Up deals with your mobile account. Check back often for new Verizon offers.

Watch our Get to Know Verizon Up video.

Learn more about Verizon Up.

About Verizon Up

Expand All

  1. What is Verizon Up?

    Welcome to Verizon Up, where you can enjoy special offers and benefits designed with you in mind, when you have a standard mobile phone account. Verizon Up consists of:

    • Best Offers: Offers personalized for you, when you consider upgrading your device, adding a line, or a new plan.
    • Featured Offers: Surprise extras and discounts from Verizon and our partners that you can claim anytime after they appear. Claim one, or claim them all. They're always changing and available to you if you are eligible.
    • Make the most of your plan: Highlights unused benefits from your Unlimited plan that are not currently activated, such as Apple Arcade速 or Disney+.
    • Super Tickets: Access to our most exclusive experiences, including sports and concerts, as well as new devices and more. Theyre first come, first served, and only a limited number of Super Tickets are made available. If you qualify, you can try to claim Super Tickets.

      Note: Learn about restrictions by visiting the Verizon Up Terms.
    • Presale tickets: Exclusive access to purchase tickets for the artists you love, before theyre available to the general public. If you qualify, you are able to purchase Presale tickets. Tickets are first come, first served, and there is a limit of 4 Presale tickets per event, unless the artist sets a lower limit.
    • Survey: Provide feedback on Verizon Up and the offers you receive, as well as express your personal preferences, so we can continue to enhance and personalize your Verizon Up experience.
    • Keep Track of it All: Find offers you've claimed and have not used, as well as view some of the services you have with Verizon.

    Verizon Up is free and available to you by signing into your account through My Verizon, if you qualify.

    Additional terms and conditions may apply to individual offers.

    Learn more about Verizon Up by visiting the Verizon Up Terms.

  2. Am I eligible for Verizon Up?

    You are eligible if you have a standard mobile account and meet all of the following requirements:

    • Are 18 or over.
    • Have a smartphone on an active standard Verizon mobile account in good standing.
    • Are a resident of the US (excluding the territories of Guam, Puerto Rico and the Virgin Islands).

    See the Verizon Up Terms for more details.

    Note: Business and prepaid accounts aren't eligible for Verizon Up at this time. Verizon Up is not available to lines associated with a Just Kids Unlimited plan, Gizmo device, a Smart Family account child line, or TLC Tab Disney Edition tablet.

  3. How do I enroll in Verizon Up?

    Enrollment is not required. You can access Verizon Up when you sign in to your My Verizon account.

    If you are already signed in:

    • My Verizon online: From the top of the Verizon home page select Why Verizon> Get More> Verizon Up.
    • My Verizon app: Select the Verizon Up icon at the bottom of your screen.

  4. Is Verizon Up available in Spanish?

    Yes, when the language on your device is set to Spanish (US), you can update your My Verizon app to the Spanish version. Additionally, you may choose the Spanish language indicator, in the top right of your screen, when viewing Verizon online.

    Note: The My Verizon app only supports Spanish - United States at this time. You'll receive an error message, stating the app isn't available in Spanish, when your device language is set to other versions of Spanish.

Redeeming an offer

Expand All

  1. How often do Verizon Up offers change?

    • Best Offers change at different times and will change depending on your recent upgrade activity.
    • Featured Offers and Super Tickets change often.
    • Presale tickets are added as they become available.

    Check back often for new offers, and to see if there are new Super Tickets available.

  2. Who can claim a Best Offer or a Featured Offer?

    Best Offers can only be claimed by an Account Owner or Account Manager.

    Featured Offers can be claimed by anyone with an eligible line on a qualifying mobile account.

    Learn more about Verizon account access roles.

  3. How do I claim a Best Offer or a Featured Offer?

    Best Offers can only be claimed by an Account Owner or Account Manager through Verizon Up. They can't be claimed over the phone or in a Verizon store.

    To claim a Best Offer:

    1. You can access Verizon Up through both the My Verizon app or My Verizon online. Sign in to your My Verizon account.

      If you are already signed in:
      • My Verizon online: From the top of the Verizon home page select Why Verizon> Get More> Verizon Up.
      • My Verizon app: Select the Verizon Up icon at the bottom of your screen.
    2. Select the offer you want to redeem.
    3. Follow the prompts to complete your purchase.

    Anyone with a line on a qualifying Verizon mobile account can claim Featured Offers by signing into your My Verizon account.

    To claim a Featured Offer:

    1. You can access Verizon Up through both the My Verizon app or My Verizon online. Sign in to your My Verizon account.

      If you are already signed in:
      • My Verizon online: From the top of the Verizon home page select Why Verizon> Get More> Verizon Up.
      • My Verizon app: Select the Verizon Up icon at the bottom of your screen.
    2. Select a Featured Offer you want.
    3. Read the details of the offer, and select Claim This.
    4. You've successfully claimed a Featured Offer. If you want to use it right away, select View Details and select Use Now.
    5. Otherwise, select Save for Later, and your Featured Offer will be saved in the Manage Claimed Offers section within Keep Track of it All.

  4. I don't see the Verizon Up offer that I saw earlier and now want to try to claim. Where did it go?

    Offers can change at any time.

    If you find an offer you like, claim it as soon as you can. You should also check the details on each offer to make sure you claim and use them before they expire.

  5. How do I use a Featured Offer once I've claimed it?

    To use a Featured offer:

    1. You can access Verizon Up through both the My Verizon app or My Verizon online. Sign in to your My Verizon account.

      If you are already signed in:
      • My Verizon online: From the top of the Verizon home page select Why Verizon> Get More> Verizon Up.
      • My Verizon app: Select the Verizon Up icon at the bottom of your screen.
    2. Scroll down to Keep Track of it All.
    3. Select Manage Claimed Offers.
    4. Select the offer you'd like to use.
    5. Follow the redemption instructions for that offer.
    6. Once you've successfully used your Featured Offer, it will no longer appear in your Manage Claimed Offers tab.

  6. Who can use a Featured Offer?

    Anyone with an eligible line on a mobile account can claim and use a Featured Offer.

    Go to the Manage Claimed Offers section, in Keep Track of it All, to see all non-used and non-expired offers that youve claimed.

  7. I've changed my mind and no longer want the offer I chose. Can I change it?

    No, once an offer is claimed, it can't be returned or exchanged.

  8. Are Verizon Up offers transferable?

    Offers aren't transferable to another account.

  9. I don't see an offer I claimed in Managed Claimed Offers. What do I do?

    Please call Verizon Up Customer Service at 866-895-5579.

Presale Tickets

Expand All

  1. What are Verizon Up Presale tickets?

    With Verizon Up, you have exclusive access to purchase tickets for the artists you love before the tickets become available to the general public. The presale period varies, but it's typically 2-3 days ahead of the regular sale period.

    You can choose your seats from select Presale tickets that are available in a variety of seating sections and prices.

  2. How do I purchase Presale tickets, and how many can I buy?

    You can browse available Presale tickets, and purchase them through the Ticketmaster website.

    If you qualify, you can purchase up to 4 Presale tickets per event, unless the artist sets a limit less than 4.

    To purchase Presale tickets:

    1. You can access Verizon Up through both the My Verizon app or My Verizon online. Sign in to your My Verizon account.

      If you are already signed in:

      • My Verizon online: From the top of the Verizon home page select Why Verizon > Get More > Verizon Up.
      • My Verizon app: Select the Verizon Up icon at the bottom of your screen.
    2. Scroll to the Exclusive Access section and select the Presale ticket window. Select the active event you're interested in.
    3. Select the date and location you prefer.
    4. Select Get tickets and follow the onscreen prompts to purchase your tickets through the Ticketmaster website.
    5. Ticketmaster will require you to sign in or create an account to purchase tickets. Youll also be prompted to connect your My Verizon account to your Ticketmaster account to unlock your access to the Verizon Up Presale tickets.

  3. I purchased Presale tickets through Verizon Up, and I have an issue with my tickets. What should I do?

    If you're experiencing an issue regarding Presale tickets you've purchased, please call Ticketmaster at 800-653-8000.

    You can also go to Ticketmaster's website for other ways to contact Ticketmaster Customer Service.

Super Tickets

Expand All

  1. How do I claim Verizon Up Super Tickets? How often can I claim Super Tickets?

    Sign into your My Verizon account to try to claim Super Tickets, if you qualify.

    If you have the Verizon Visa Card, you may see additional exclusive Super Tickets that you can try to claim.

    1. You can access Verizon Up through both the My Verizon app or My Verizon online. Sign in to your My Verizon account.

      If you are already signed in:
      • My Verizon online: From the top of the Verizon home page select Why Verizon > Get More > Verizon Up.
      • My Verizon app: Select the Verizon Up icon at the bottom of your screen.
    2. Locate the Exclusive Access section.
    3. Select the Super Ticket you want to try to claim.
    4. Read the details carefully to make sure you understand what the Super Ticket is and when you need to use it.
    5. When the countdown clock reaches 0, select Try to Claim.
    6. You must submit the claim form before the timer reaches zero.

    Super Tickets are first come, first served, and quantities are limited. If prompted, be sure to complete the claim forms right away (before the timer on the screen reaches 0), or you may lose the Super Ticket.

    Once a Super Ticket is claimed, no one on your account will be able to claim another for 6 months. If you claim a High Value Super Ticket, no one on your account will be able to claim another for 24 months.

  2. How do I receive my Verizon Up Super Tickets?

    Timing may vary, but generally you'll receive a confirmation email within 5 - 7 business days with details about receiving the Super Tickets.

    The way you receive the actual tickets will also vary by event. You could receive a PDF, digital ticket, Will Call ticket, etc.

  3. I thought I claimed Verizon Up Super Tickets, but now they're all claimed. Can I still get them?

    Because Super Tickets are available on a first come, first served basis, it's important that you fill out the claim forms promptly. You must:

    • Select Try to Claim for the ticket you want, once the countdown clock reaches 0.
    • Complete the claim form before the timer is up.

    If you don't complete the claim form before the timer reaches 0, you may lose the Super Tickets.

  4. Are there any restrictions on Super Tickets?

    The following restrictions may apply:

    • Limited quantity.
    • Tickets are first, come first served, and can be claimed after the countdown clock reaches 0.
    • Employees may or may not be eligible. Terms specify if an employee is eligible.
    • If you qualify, you can redeem 1 Super Ticket every 6 months, per each mobile phone account.
    • If you qualify, you can claim 1 High Value Super Ticket every 24 months (e.g., a Fly Away ticket), per each mobile phone account. (After claiming a High Value Super Ticket, you must wait 6 months before you are eligible to claim a Super Ticket.)

    A High Value Super Ticket is a Super Ticket with a total value of $600 or higher (i.e., a Super Ticket that meets the 1099 tax threshold).

    Any applicable federal, state and/or local income and other taxes on the Super Ticket are your sole responsibility. We may be required to report taxable amounts to the appropriate taxing authority.

  5. Can I resell Super Tickets or other Verizon Up rewards I've claimed?

    No, you can't resell, transfer or give away Super Tickets or other offers. We may suspend or cancel your access to Verizon Up if you sell a Verizon Up offer.

  6. I entered the wrong details when claiming my Verizon Up Super Tickets. What should I do?

    Please call Verizon Up Customer Service at 866-895-5579.

Additional questions

Expand All

  1. What happened to my Device Dollars?

    Verizon Up has changed. As part of the changes, all unused Device Dollars expired on 6/30/22 and are no longer available to be used toward the purchase of a device.

    If you have a Verizon Visa Card, you may continue to earn Verizon Dollars. Please visit our Verizon Visa Card FAQs.

  2. Do I get extra rewards if I have the Verizon Visa Card?

    Verizon mobile Account Owners or Account Managers of a Verizon mobile account with up to 12 phone lines (depending on plan) can apply for the Verizon Visa Card.

    If approved:

    • You can earn Verizon Dollars when you make purchases1 everywhere Visa cards are accepted.
    • Verizon Dollars2 can be used to pay your Verizon mobile or Fios Home Internet bills3 or buy devices and accessories.

    Learn more about Verizon Dollars on our Verizon Visa Card FAQs page.

    1Purchases subject to Credit approval. See the Verizon Visa Card Rewards Program Terms & Conditions for details and restrictions.
    2Verizon Dollars have no cash value, are issued for reward purposes only and cannot be purchased, sold, transferred, redeemed for cash or Verizon gift cards, or used for security deposits for Verizon wireless or Verizon Fios account services.
    3Fios customers must be enrolled in the Mobile + Home Discount program to access Verizon Up and to apply Verizon Dollars to the Verizon Fios bill.

  3. Where can I locate my TravelPass days?

    TravelPass days that are included with your Unlimited plan and/or Verizon Visa credit card will display in the Keep Track of it All section within the Verizon Up tab in the My Verizon App. At this time, TravelPasses can only be seen and managed in the My Verizon App and not on the Verizon website.

    Select Manage Your TravelPass days to see your TravelPass bank, where you will find your available TravelPasses, as well as additional information about TravelPass.

    Visit our Travel Pass FAQs to learn more.

  4. Where can I locate my Mobile + Home Discount information?

    Your Mobile + Home Discount program status can be accessed in the Keep Track of it All section.

    You can upgrade or manage your Mobile + Home Discount in Verizon Up, or visit the Savings & Promos center to make changes.

  5. Where do I locate my Verizon Visa Card Info?

    Your Verizon Visa Card information can be accessed in the Keep Track of it All section, where your Verizon Dollar balance and your recent Verizon Visa Card activity will be displayed.

  6. Who can I call with questions about my Verizon Up account?

    For general questions related to Verizon Up please call us at 866-895-5579.

    • Mon - Fri: 8 AM - 8 PM ET
    • Sat - Sun: 8 AM - 6 PM ET

    For questions specific to Verizon Dollars, please visit Contact Us.
