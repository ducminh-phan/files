Verizon Visa® Card FAQs – earn Verizon Dollars

Learn how to apply for the Verizon Visa Card and if approved, use it to earn Verizon Dollars to pay your Verizon Wireless or Fios Home Internet bills or buy devices and accessories, book travel, and purchase merchant gift cards.

  • Am I eligible to apply for the Verizon Visa Card?
  • I have a Verizon Wireless account. How do I apply for the Verizon Visa Card?
  • How do I earn Verizon Dollars with my Verizon Visa Card?
  • Can I add my Verizon Visa Card to digital wallets?
  • Who can I contact if I have questions about my Verizon Visa Card bill, service or if I lose my card?
  • How can I qualify for promotions when I use my Verizon Visa Card?

About the Verizon Visa Card

Use the card and earn rewards on every purchase, everywhere your Verizon Visa Card is accepted. Buy gas, shop at grocery stores, dine out or order-in and more, and earn Verizon Dollars1 to redeem toward Verizon purchases or to use toward your monthly Verizon Wireless or Fios bill2 to book travel on airlines, hotels, rental cars, or buy merchant gift cards.

    • No annual or foreign transaction fees.5
    • 2 free TravelPass days per calendar year (a $20 value). TravelPass eligibility rules apply.

    When you open a Verizon Visa Card account, and make your first purchase using your new card within 90 days, you will get a $100 statement credit applied to your credit card bill.

  6. When I use the Verizon Visa Card app with my Apple iPhone, I can't open links. How do I fix this?

    If the Safari browser is disabled in your device's settings, you may not be able to open any links within the Verizon Visa Card app or My Verizon app. Here's how to activate Safari if it is disabled:

    3. Tap Content & Privacy Restrictions.
    4. Tap Allowed Apps.
    5. Tap the toggle next to Safari to enable the browser.

Verizon Visa Card Eligibility

Expand All

  1. Am I eligible to apply for the Verizon Visa Card?

    You must be a Verizon Wireless customer who is the Account Owner or an Account Manager and have 12 phone lines maximum (depending on plan) on your wireless account to be eligible to apply for the Verizon Visa Card.

    Verizon Business accounts and Prepaid accounts aren’t eligible to apply for the Verizon Visa Card.

  2. Can I apply for the Verizon Visa Card if I'm a Verizon Fios customer?

    You must be a Verizon Wireless customer who is the Account Owner or an Account Manager and have 12 phone lines maximum (depending on plan) on your account to apply for the Verizon Visa Card.

    Verizon Business accounts and Prepaid accounts aren't eligible to apply for the Verizon Visa Card.

  3. Can more than 1 person on my Verizon Wireless account apply for the Verizon Visa Card?

    Yes. Account Owners and Account Managers on your Verizon Wireless account can each apply for the Verizon Visa Card.

    The following applies for all Verizon Visa Card holders on the same Verizon Wireless account:

    • All earned Verizon Dollars1 will accumulate together in your shared Verizon Dollar Bank in My Verizon website and My Verizon app, and on the Verizon Visa Card app.
    • Verizon Dollars can only be redeemed by the Account Owner or an Account Manager.

    1 Verizon Dollars have no cash value and are issued for reward purposes only and cannot be purchased, sold, transferred, redeemed for cash or Verizon gift cards, or used for security deposits for Verizon Wireless or Verizon Fios account services.

  4. Can there be more than 1 person on my credit card account?

    Yes. Account Owners who are the primary card holders can add authorized users to their credit card account.

    The following applies for all card holders on the same credit card account:

    • The primary card holder will be liable for all purchases made on the credit card account, including those made by authorized users.
    • All earned Verizon Dollars1 will accumulate together in your shared Verizon Dollar Bank in My Verizon website and My Verizon app, and on the Verizon Visa Card app.
    • Verizon Dollars can only be redeemed by the Account Owner or an Account Manager on your Verizon Wireless account.

    1 Verizon Dollars have no cash value and are issued for reward purposes only and cannot be purchased, sold, transferred, redeemed for cash or Verizon gift cards, or used for security deposits for Verizon Wireless or Verizon Fios account services.

  6. Can I use my Verizon Visa Card as an Auto Pay method for my Verizon Wireless or Fios bill?

    Yes, you can use the Verizon Visa Card to enroll in Auto Pay for your Verizon bill payment.

    Verizon Wireless bills:

    • If you are a new enrollee to Verizon's Auto Pay1 and paper-free billing, you can receive up to $10/mo off each eligible Verizon plan account or line, up to 12 lines maximum, depending on plan. Offer is for first-time enrollees. For more details, visit Verizon Wireless Auto Pay FAQs.
    • If you are a current Auto Pay enrollee, you will not receive additional Auto Pay discounts for re-enrolling using the Verizon Visa Card but will continue to earn 2% in Verizon Dollars when you pay your monthly bill using your card.

    Verizon Fios home internet bills:

    • When you use the Verizon Visa Card as your Auto Pay method along with paper-free billing, you can get $10/month off your Fios bill.2 Learn more about Auto Pay for your Fios bill.

    You need to sign in to My Verizon and enroll in Auto Pay with your Verizon Visa Card to receive the monthly discount on eligible accounts and plan lines. If you’re already enrolled in Auto Pay and getting a monthly discount, sign in to My Verizon to add your Verizon Visa Card and earn 2% in Verizon Dollars.3

    1 Enrollment in Verizon’s Auto Pay and paper-free billing is required for Verizon bill discount of up to $10/mo off each eligible account or line, up to 12 lines maximum, depending on plan. Visit Auto Pay FAQs for more info. Please note: Offer is for first-time enrollees. Verizon customers currently enrolled in Auto Pay and paper-free billing via other methods (e.g., ACH or debit card) will not receive additional discounts for re-enrolling using the card.

    2 Fios customers must be enrolled in the Mobile + Home Discount program to access the Verizon Up Rewards Center and to apply earned Verizon Dollars to the Verizon Fios bill.

    3 Purchases subject to credit approval. See the Verizon Visa Credit Card Rewards Program Terms and Conditions for details and restrictions.

  7. How can enrolling in the Mobile + Home Discount program offer more ways to pay and earn rewards with my Verizon Visa Card?

    You will have the opportunity to enroll your Verizon Visa Card in the Mobile + Home Discount program and earn 2% in rewards on your monthly bill.

    Simply sign in to your My Fios and My Verizon Wireless accounts, then navigate to the Savings and Promotions Center. By selecting Yes when prompted to save your Verizon Visa Card during the Mobile + Home Discount enrollment, your Verizon Visa Card (or any future Verizon Visa Cards) will be saved to both of the linked My Fios and My Verizon Wireless accounts as a saved payment option for future purchases and bill payments by the respective Account Owner or Account Managers.

    To de-enroll your Verizon Visa Card from the Mobile + Home Discount program, you will need to access the My Verizon app, and navigate to the Savings & Promotions Center.

    Visit our Mobile + Home Discount FAQs to learn more.

How to apply for the Verizon Visa Card

Expand All

  1. I have a Verizon Wireless account. How do I apply for the Verizon Visa Card?

    Verizon Wireless customers who are Account Owners or Account Managers with up to 12 phone lines maximum (depending on plan), on their wireless account can apply anytime:

    • On our website: Visit our Verizon Visa Card page
    • In the My Verizon app: Tap the menu icon at the top of the screen and choose Verizon Visa Card.
    • At checkout on our website or app: Choose the Apply link when you see it during checkout.
    • Over the phone with our Customer Service
    • In a Verizon store or at select Verizon Authorized Retailers

    Verizon Business accounts and Prepaid accounts aren't eligible to apply for the Verizon Visa Card.

  2. How do I apply for the card if I’m not a Verizon Wireless customer yet?

    If you don’t yet have a Verizon Wireless account, you can apply for the Verizon Visa Card after you have established yourself as a Verizon Account Owner or Account Manager.

    You must be a Verizon Wireless customer who is the Account Owner or an Account Manager and have up to 12 phone lines maximum (depending on plan) on your wireless account to be eligible to apply for the Verizon Visa Card.

    Verizon Business accounts and Prepaid accounts aren’t eligible to apply for the Verizon Visa Card.

    Once you've met these eligibility requirements, visit our Verizon Visa Card page to get started.

  3. Can I apply for the Verizon Visa Card in a Verizon store or by phone?

    Yes. Verizon Wireless customers who are Account Owners and Account Managers with up to 12 phone lines maximum (depending on plan) on their account can apply for the Verizon Visa Card at a Verizon store, at select Verizon Authorized Retailers, by phone and in other ways .

  4. Who do I contact for help with my Verizon Visa Card application?

    If you are experiencing a technical issue with your online application, you can visit verizonvisacard.syf.com to restart your application.

    If you did not receive an immediate approval decision, please wait for your letter from Synchrony Bank or contact Verizon Visa Card customer service at 866-313-9396.

  5. Can I use my Verizon Visa Card right away if I'm approved?

    Yes, if you're instantly approved at a Verizon operated store or Verizon website, you can use your new Verizon Visa Card right away in the following ways:

    • At the time of credit card approval, your new card will be automatically added to your Verizon Wireless account as your preferred method of payment for that day's purchase and future purchases at Verizon operated stores or at Verizon online.
    • You will be able to select another form of payment on a per-transaction basis at the point of sale.
    • When making a Verizon purchase, choose Verizon Visa Card as your payment method. You should set your Verizon Visa Card as your preferred payment method in My Verizon on our website or the My Verizon app to make sure you earn 2% in Rewards on Verizon purchases.
    • If you're also a Verizon Fios customer, you can manually add the credit card to your Fios account as a preferred payment method.

    Yes, if you're instantly approved at an exclusive Verizon Authorized Retailer you can use your Verizon Visa Card in the following ways:

    • At the time of credit approval, you can use a Temporary Shopping Pass displayed on your device’s screen immediately. Or, print your Temporary Shopping Pass from the email sent from Synchrony Bank.

    You cannot use your Verizon Visa Card anywhere other than described above until you receive your permanent card from Synchrony Bank, which should arrive in 7-10 days. Please make sure you activate your Verizon Visa Card upon arrival.

Receiving your Verizon Visa Card and Accessibility

Expand All

  1. How long will it take to get my Verizon Visa Card in the mail after I’m approved?

    You should get your card and your credit limit information in 7-10 business days. Activate your credit card online or by calling the 800 number on the back of your card immediately to be sure your full credit limit is available to you.

  2. How will my Verizon Visa Card arrive?

    Depending on which card you are approved for – Verizon Visa Card or Verizon Visa Signature Card – your card will arrive in the following manner:

    The Verizon Visa Card will arrive in an 8.5 x 11-inch standard white card carrier. The card is attached to the carrier. Remove from the card from the carrier and follow the activation instructions.

    The Verizon Visa Signature Card will arrive in a 7-inch x 4-inch x .75-inch box with embossed text that reads "It's for you" across the top. The card itself will be within a cardboard sleeve inside the box. Remove the card from the sleeve and follow the activation instructions.

    Verizon Visa Signature Card box and sleeve >

  3. How do I activate my Verizon Visa Card?

    To activate your Verizon Visa Card, visit verizonvisacard.syf.com/activate or call:

    • Verizon Visa Card – (866) 440-8047
    • Verizon Visa Signature Card – (866) 647-1096

  4. How do I retrieve my card from the sleeve?

    To retrieve your card from the cardboard sleeve:

    1. Orient the credit card box vertically with the tear tab to your right.
    2. Pull the tear tab down towards your body and then lift the lid open like a book.
    3. At the top of the open box (the side farthest away from your body), locate the access hole or lift tab.
    4. Using the hole, remove the cardboard sleeve from within the box.

      Note: The sleeve can be identified by the embossed Verizon checkmark located on the bottom left of the sleeve when the package is oriented vertically.
    5. With one hand gripping the bottom of the sleeve containing the checkmark, and your other hand gripping the top portion of the sleeve, gently pull the sleeve apart.
    6. Your card is inside a textured envelope.

  5. How can I identify my Verizon Visa Card from other cards in my wallet?

    • Depending on which card you were approved for and received:
      • Verizon Visa Card is made out of plastic and is industry standard.
      • Verizon Visa Signature Card is made out of metal and is also industry standard.
    • Both card types have an embossed Verizon checkmark on the front of the card in the lower left hand corner when the card is oriented vertically with the chip farthest from your body.
    • Your Verizon Visa Card number is located on the back of the card in raised print.

  6. Who can I contact if I have questions about physically accessing or using my Verizon Visa Card?

    We want to make sure that every customer has the chance to get the very most from their Verizon Visa Card, regardless of ability. If you have questions about physically accessing or using your card, please contact Synchrony Bank at 1-866-313-9396.

  7. How do I use my Verizon Visa Card for contactless payment at a pay terminal?

    Your Verizon Visa Card is a contactless credit card. You will find the Contactless indicator on the back of your card.

    When using your Verizon Visa Card for in-store purchases, look for the Contactless payments symbol at the checkout terminal. Hold your card 1-2 inches above the Contactless payments symbol on the checkout terminal for 1-2 seconds to complete your payment.

    Learn more about Contactless Payment with Visa.

    The Contactless Symbol and Contactless Indicator are trademarks owned by and used with permission of EMVCo, LCC.

  8. How do I properly orient the Verizon Visa Card for use at a pay terminal?

    Your vertically oriented Verizon Visa Card includes a chip located on the front of the card towards the upper right corner.

    When inserting the card into a pay terminal, make sure the card is oriented with the chip facing the ceiling and farthest away from your body.

    Plus, you can tap to pay with your contactless Verizon Visa Card for in-store purchases where you see the Contactless Symbol. Learn more about Contactless Payment with Visa.

    When tapping to pay, the terminal reads the antenna best when your contactless card is flat over the Contactless Symbol, versus at an angle.

Earning & using Verizon Dollars

Expand All

  1. How do I earn Verizon Dollars with my Verizon Visa Card?

    When you're approved for the Verizon Visa Card you'll start earning rewards on qualifying purchases as part of the Verizon Visa Card Rewards Program.

    When you use the Verizon Visa Card just as you would any other credit card, you earn a percentage of the purchase in Verizon Dollars1:

    • 4% on gas and grocery store purchases
    • 3% on dining purchases, including take-out and delivery
    • 2% on Verizon purchases
    • 1% on all other purchases where Visa credit cards are accepted.

    For example, if you spend $100 at a grocery store using your Verizon Visa Card, you automatically earn 4% in Verizon Dollars. There's no limit on the amount of Verizon Dollars you can earn for purchases.2

    Learn more with the Verizon Visa Credit Card Rewards Program Terms and Conditions.

    1Purchases subject to credit approval. Verizon Dollars will be forfeited for Rewards Program inactivity.
    2 Transactions made within other establishments (i.e., supercenters) may not qualify. We do not evaluate whether merchants correctly identify and bill transactions; however we do reserve the right to determine which transactions qualify for Verizon Dollars.

  2. How do I view the Verizon Dollars I earn with my Verizon Visa Card?

    You can track your Verizon Dollar earnings, activity and to redeem:

    • My Verizon app or website. The Verizon Visa Card Dashboard (requires account sign-in).
    • Verizon Visa Card app. Download from your devices app store.
    • My Verizon app. Verizon Up Rewards Center. Scroll down the page to Keep track of it all to see your Verizon Dollar balance.

  3. When are my Verizon Dollars available to use?

    Your Verizon Dollars will be available within 1-2 Verizon Visa Card billing cycles. You will see the amount of Verizon Dollars you've earned posted to your monthly credit card billing statement. You can also see how many Verizon Dollars you have available, as well as how many you earned on a daily basis, in your digital wallet, which is available through the Verizon Visa Card app, the My Verizon app, and in the Verizon Visa Card dashboard on My Verizon app or online.

  4. Who on my Verizon Visa Card account can earn rewards and redeem Verizon Dollars?

    Earn: Verizon Wireless Account Owners and Account Managers can apply for the Verizon Visa Card and if approved, can earn on every purchase, everywhere your Verizon Visa Card is accepted1. Verizon Dollars2 earned by any card holder on the same Verizon Visa Card account will accumulate together in your shared Verizon Dollar Bank in the Verizon Visa Card Dashboard in My Verizon app and website, and the Verizon Visa Card app.

    Redeem: Verizon Wireless Account Owners or Account Managers can redeem Verizon Dollars and can claim rewards.

    If your Verizon Wireless account access role changes, your access to the digital wallet of Verizon Dollars may be affected. For example, if you are no longer a Verizon Wireless Account Owner or Verizon Wireless Account Manager, you will not be permitted to access the digital wallet of Verizon Dollars, even if you are a card holder.

    Learn how to add or change an Account Owner or Account Manager.

    1Purchases subject to credit approval. Verizon Dollars will be forfeited for Rewards Program Inactivity. See the Verizon Visa® Credit Card Rewards Program Terms & Conditions for details and restrictions.

    If you later return a purchase for which you earned Verizon Dollars, those earned Verizon Dollars will be removed from your Verizon Dollar balance.
