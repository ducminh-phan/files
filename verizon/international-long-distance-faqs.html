International Long Distance FAQs

Learn about calling internationally from the US. using the Verizon mobile network. Find out about our Global Calling Plus, Global Calling and Global Choice plans making international calls and blocking international numbers.

For information about plans for traveling outside the US, visit International Services (while outside the US).

For prepaid plans, go to our Prepaid International Calling FAQs for information.

About International Calling

Expand All

  1. What is International Calling while in the US?

    While you're in the US, you can make calls to another country using your device. If you call other countries only occasionally, you can pay a standard per-minute rate.

    If you make such calls frequently, consider getting an international calling plan:

    International calling plan Which countries and minutes are included & how to add the plan
    Global Calling Plus (formerly Unlimited Together World) Enjoy unlimited long distance calls from the US to landline phones in over 60 countries and to mobile devices in over 30 countries. Plus, make discounted calls to an additional 160+ destinations - all for $15/month per line.

    See countries included with Global Calling Plus.

    Add the Global Calling Plus plan to your device's line in My Verizon online, in the My Verizon app or by texting the word Plus to 4004.
    Global Calling (formerly Unlimited Together - North America) Enjoy unlimited long distance calls from the US to Mexico and Canada for just $5/month per line. And, make discounted calls to an additional 220+ destinations.

    See countries included with Global Calling.

    Add the Global Calling plan to your mobile device's line in My Verizon online, in the My Verizon app or by texting the word Global to 4004.
    Global Choice Pick a country and get up to 300 minutes/month depending on the selected Global Choice country. You can add up to 93 countries for $10/line per month for each country. Get discounted rates after the allowances to the selected country. Plus, make discounted calls from $0.05/per minute to 220+ countries. And get unlimited minutes to Mexico and Canada.

    Add Global Choice to your mobile device's line in My Verizon online, in the My Verizon app or by texting the country's keyword to 4004.

    You can find the list of country keywords here.

    Notes:
    • Any existing line level international long distance plan you have will be removed when you add Global Choice.
    • Any existing account level international plan will be removed from all lines when you add Global Choice to any line on the account.

    For more details about calling other countries, visit our International Services (while in the US) page and enter a country name to get calling and messaging rate options and dialing instructions.

    Note:

    • All Unlimited plans provide unlimited calling to Mexico and Canada. Plus you can take your domestic talk, text and data allowances with you to Mexico and Canada at no additional charge. Learn more about our Unlimited plan options.
    • Making international calls while in the US to certain countries may be restricted for a variety of reasons. However, you can always place an international call from your mobile device using a calling card.

  5. What's the difference between a landline terminating charge and a mobile terminating charge?

    The charge for a call or message from your mobile device to a landline is called a landline terminating charge. The charge for a call or to send a message from your mobile device to a mobile number is called a mobile terminating charge.

    The charge for an international call while in the US includes the airtime charge from your plan, in addition to the per-minute rate based on what type of phone answered the call.

  6. If someone calls me while they're outside the US, will I be billed the international rate?

    No, you won't be charged international rates if you're in the US and someone calls you while they're outside the US.

  7. How do I block/unblock calls to international numbers?

    To block all international calls, please call 611 and speak to one of our representatives. Calls to Puerto Rico, US Virgin Islands, Guam and Northern Marianas are not international.

    To allow international calls, you can add an international calling plan on the Change Your Plan page in My Verizon.

    If you subscribe to Verizon Smart Family, you can block calling specific international numbers. For more information, view the Verizon Smart Family FAQs. Not subscribed to Verizon Smart Family? Sign up for Verizon Smart Family today.

    Note: On the Just Kids plan, calls to everyone except trusted contacts is blocked.

  3. How do I block an international phone number?

    You can use our free number blocking service to block calls and messages from domestic and international numbers from countries that follow the 10-digit North American numbering plan. If you subscribe to Verizon Smart Family or Usage Controls, you can block any international number.

    Visit our Block Numbers page to learn how to block numbers and more about these services.

Making an international call from the US

Expand All

  1. Am I able to make international calls from my Verizon mobile device?

    Yes, we offer 2 different features, International Services Enabled Lite and International Services Enabled, which let you make international calls from your Verizon mobile device. International Services Enabled Lite is included with your plan and lets you make international calls from the US to all countries, except:

    Countries  
    Angola Ascension Island
    Azerbaijan Congo, Dem. Republic of
    Congo, Republic of Djibouti
    East Timor Estonia
    Gambia Guinea
    Latvia Liberia
    Lithuania Maldives
    Mayotte Senegal
    Sierra Leone St. Helena

    To make calls from the US to any of these 18 countries, or to make calls to other countries while traveling outside the US (except for calls back to the US or calls within the country in which you're traveling), you must add International Services Enabled to your account. To add International Services Enabled to your account, your account must have been active for at least 34 days and be in good standing. If your account has been active for less than 34 days, contact Customer Service so that we can determine if you're eligible.

  2. How can I get help making international calls from the US?

    If you need help making international calls from the US, please visit our International Service Options (while in the US) page. You'll also find more information about requirements, rates and coverage.

    For information about plans and rates while traveling outside the US, visit our International Services page or Trip Planner tool.

  3. How do I place an international call while in the US with my Verizon mobile device?

    To place a call while in the US to a country that uses 10-digit dialing, simply enter the 10-digit phone number you wish to call, then tap the Call button.

    To place a call while in the US to all other countries, you'll start with 011. Next, you must enter the country code, followed by the number you wish to reach.

    For example, if you'd like to call a number in France:

    1. Enter 011 and the country code 33.
    2. Enter the phone number.
    3. Tap the Call button.

    For more information about dialing specific countries, visit our International Service Options (while in the US) page.

    For information about placing calls while traveling outside the US, visit our Trip Planner tool.

About International Messaging

Expand All

  1. How much does international messaging cost while I'm in the US?

    These plans include unlimited messaging to and from other countries while you're in the US:

    • Current Unlimited plans: One Unlimited for iPhone, Welcome Unlimited, 5G Start, 5G Play More, 5G Do More, 5G Get More
    • Shared Data 5GB* and Shared Data 10GB*
    • The Verizon plan shared data plans (e.g., S, M, L)*
    • Get More Unlimited*, Do More Unlimited*, Play More Unlimited*, Above Unlimited*, Beyond Unlimited*, Go Unlimited*, Just Kids*

    If you're on a different plan, you can view international messaging rates on our International Services (while in the US) page.

    Note: On the Just Kids plan, messaging to everyone except trusted contacts is blocked.

    *No longer available to add to your account.

  2. Do I need to sign up for international messaging?

    International messaging is automatically included when you enable international services on your account.

  3. Can I send picture and video messages (multimedia messages) to international destinations from the US?

    International multimedia message (MMS) coverage is available for certain countries and mobile operators within those countries.

    To check availability by country, go to our International Services (while in the US) page, and enter a country in the search field. You'll see the multimedia messaging services available for that country.

  4. What number do I need to use to send an international message?

    To send an international message while in the US:

    • To a country that uses 10-digit dialing: Just use the 10-digit phone number
    • To all other countries: Enter 011, then the country code of the country you're texting, followed by the number you want to message.

    For example, if you'd like to text a number in France:

    1. Enter 011.
    2. Enter the country code 33.
    3. Enter the phone number you want to text message.

    For additional information on dialing patterns for specific countries, visit our International Service Options (while in the US) page.
