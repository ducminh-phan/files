INTERNATIONAL

Overview

YETI ships internationally to select countries. To access the International country selector, or for a list of available shipping countries and shopping currencies, click on the upper left-hand corner of the website to select your flag. Once a country is selected, product prices will appear in the local currency and order totals will be populated to include international shipping costs. Most products can be shipped internationally, with some exceptions including custom or licensed product.


We are unable to accept payment cards with international billing addresses on orders shipping to the U.S.


We no longer service Mexico, Japan and United Arab Emirates through our YETI.com website. You can order authentic YETI products directly through authorized local dealers.


Holiday Promotions

YETI's holiday promotions are only available on purchases delivered within the United States. For more details please visit our Holiday Shipping and Promotions page.


Can my order be delivered internationally?

Yes, it can, although certain products are excluded from international delivery. As an international shopper, you now get the benefit of seeing product prices in your local currency, guaranteed order totals at checkout with no hidden fees, cost-effective international shipping, and much more. .


Can customized product be delivered internationally?

No, it cannot. We are working to expand our offerings to include the ability to send customized orders internationally, but at this time we aren't able to ship any customized orders outside of the United States.


How much duty and taxes are charged on international orders?

Duties (or Customs tariffs) are set by the destination country Customs authorities and determined based on a combination of the country of origin (manufacturing) of the goods being purchased and the classification of that merchandise in accordance with a harmonized system.


Are there any items that cannot ship to my country because they are restricted?

If for some reason there are any items that cannot be imported into your destination country selection these items will not be available to add to the cart while shopping with the destination country’s flag selected in the upper-left corner.


Custom and licensed products are restricted and cannot ship internationally at this time.


Do you ship to military addresses?

Orders shipping to military addresses should go through our domestic checkout, as they are not subject to the same costs and logistics flows as our other international orders. To do this, you should shop as if shipping to an address within the United States.


Can I purchase or redeem international gift cards?

We do not currently support the sale of gift cards to international customers or the redemption of gift cards for international orders.


Can I register for an account?

YETI does not allow for international addresses in our accounts. Please use a U.S. address for account set up.


I have a Pro Account on YETI.com, will my discount apply to international orders?

Unfortunately no. We’re unable to honor promotional codes or discounts on international orders.


Can I register my YETI product?

YETI does not allow for international addresses in our registration system. Your YETI product is still covered by the YETI Warranty even without registering the product online.


International Returns and Exchanges

YETI offers ground shipping on orders shipped in the contiguous United States only. Should you wish to place an order on YETI.com and use a freight forwarding company to ship products to an international address, YETI is unable to assist in this process and is not liable for any damages incurred during shipping to the final destination.


TUNDRA, TANK, AND LOADOUT

YETI only accepts returns for Tundra, TANK, and LoadOut products damaged during shipment to the US address indicated on the order and warranty issues. The YETI Tundra, TANK, and LoadOut come with a 5-year manufacturing defect warranty.


HOPPER AND PANGA

YETI only accepts returns for Hopper and Panga products damaged during shipment to the US address indicated on the order and warranty issues. The YETI Hopper and Panga come with a 3-year manufacturing defect warranty.


RAMBLER

YETI only accepts returns for Rambler products damaged during shipment to the US address indicated on the order and warranty issues. The YETI Rambler series come with a 3-year manufacturing defect warranty.


GEAR & ACCESSORIES

YETI only accepts returns for items damaged during shipment to the US address indicated on the order and warranty issues. T-shirts and hats are not protected by YETI warranty.


International Dealers

We have multiple partners around the world that can accommodate local requests with a more personal touch. Please see our list of authorized local dealers.
